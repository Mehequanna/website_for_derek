About Section

Derek Sherman Consulting has been around for over 10 years servicing local Keller Williams (KW) Offices and their agents. Derek roams from Gresham to Beaverton to Tualitan to sustain KW offices' technology. He is even known to travel as far as Eugene! He works for other local shops as well if needed. His main focus is network management, phone and network infrastructure setup, and Tech support for those who need it.

Derek Sherman Consulting focuses on the 3 Cs of technology. Consistency, Derek makes sure to consistenly visit each office every week to continually update or service networks and computers. This brings us to the next C, Continuity.

Continuity in technology is important! With the consistent servicing of the network and machines, continuity is very rarely broken in your technlogy world. You may even ask if he is "doing anything."

The last C is Cost Management. Sure you can go out and buy the newest networking devices. Derek has been doing this so long he knows where to look for 1 year old tech that costs 1/10 of the price of something new. With his knowledge, he is able to keep costs to a minimum!
